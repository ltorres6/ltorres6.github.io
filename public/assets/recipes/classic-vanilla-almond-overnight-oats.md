# Classic Vanilla Almond Overnight Oats

A simple and delicious overnight oats recipe with the classic combination of vanilla and almond flavors. This protein-rich breakfast is perfect for busy mornings and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

## Ingredients
- Rolled oats: 1/2 cup  
- Unsweetened almond milk: 1/2 cup  
- Greek yogurt: 1/2 cup  
- Chia seeds: 1 tbsp  
- Vanilla extract: 1 tsp  
- Almond butter: 1 tbsp  
- Honey or maple syrup: 1 tsp (optional)  
- Sliced almonds and fresh berries: For topping (optional)

## Instructions
1. In a mason jar or container, combine rolled oats, almond milk, Greek yogurt, chia seeds, vanilla extract, almond butter, and honey or maple syrup if using.  
2. Stir well to ensure all ingredients are evenly distributed.  
3. Cover and refrigerate overnight (at least 12 hours).  
4. In the morning, stir the oats and add sliced almonds and fresh berries before serving.

## Notes
- This recipe makes one serving, perfect for meal prep
- Can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days
- Adjust sweetness to taste with honey or maple syrup
- Try different nut butters for variety (peanut, cashew, etc.)
- Great source of protein, fiber, and healthy fats
