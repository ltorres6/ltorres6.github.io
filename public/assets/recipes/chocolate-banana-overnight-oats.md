# Chocolate Banana Overnight Oats

A delicious and nutritious make-ahead breakfast that combines the rich flavors of chocolate and banana. Perfect for busy mornings when you want something healthy and satisfying ready to go.

## Ingredients
- Rolled oats: 1/2 cup
- Unsweetened almond milk: 1/2 cup
- Greek yogurt: 1/2 cup
- Chia seeds: 1 tbsp
- Cocoa powder: 1 tbsp
- Maple syrup: 1 tbsp
- Ripe banana: 1/5 of a banana, mashed (about 1/5 of a medium banana)
- Dark chocolate chips and banana slices: For topping (optional)

## Instructions
1. In a mason jar or container, combine rolled oats, almond milk, Greek yogurt, chia seeds, cocoa powder, maple syrup, and mashed banana.
2. Mix thoroughly until all ingredients are well incorporated.
3. Cover and refrigerate overnight (at least 12 hours).
4. In the morning, give the oats a good stir and top with dark chocolate chips and banana slices before serving.

## Notes
- This recipe makes one serving, perfect for meal prep
- Can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days
- Feel free to adjust sweetness by adding more or less maple syrup
- For extra protein, add a scoop of your favorite protein powder
