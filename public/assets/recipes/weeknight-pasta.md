# Simple Weeknight Pasta

A quick and delicious pasta dish perfect for busy weeknights. Ready in under 30 minutes with ingredients you probably already have in your pantry.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb pasta (penne or rigatoni)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- Red pepper flakes to taste
- Salt and black pepper

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.
2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet.
3. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cook for 1 minute.
4. Add diced tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Drain pasta and add to the skillet.
6. Toss with fresh basil and parmesan cheese.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
8. Serve immediately.

## Notes

This recipe is very flexible - add whatever vegetables you have on hand. Spinach, zucchini, or bell peppers work great. For protein, try adding cooked chicken or Italian sausage.
